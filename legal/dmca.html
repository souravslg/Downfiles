DMCA Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Our Position

DownFiles respects intellectual property rights and expects its users to do the same. DownFiles is a tool that facilitates access to publicly available media — it does not host, store, or distribute any copyrighted content.

No Content Storage

DownFiles does not host, cache, or store any media content. All content flows directly from the original source (e.g., YouTube, TikTok) to the end user. We have no control over what content any third-party platform makes available.

DMCA Takedown Requests

If you believe that your copyrighted work is being made available through DownFiles in a way that constitutes copyright infringement, please note that the appropriate party to contact is the original platform hosting the content (e.g., YouTube, TikTok, Instagram).

For concerns specific to DownFiles, contact us via our Contact page and include:

  • Identification of the copyrighted work
  • The URL where the content is allegedly infringing
  • Your contact information
  • A statement of good faith belief

Counter-Notification

If you believe your content was wrongly removed, you may submit a counter-notification with appropriate legal documentation.
