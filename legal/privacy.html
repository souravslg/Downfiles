Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

DownFiles collects minimal data. Specifically:

  • URLs submitted: Temporarily processed to fetch media information. Not stored.
  • Server logs: Standard web server logs (IP address, timestamp) retained for up to 7 days for security purposes.

2. What We Don't Collect

  • No account or personal information
  • No downloaded file content
  • No tracking cookies or advertising data

3. Cookies

DownFiles uses minimal cookies necessary for site functionality only. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

4. Third-Party Services

When you submit a URL, DownFiles connects to the target platform (e.g., YouTube, TikTok) to retrieve media information. Your IP may be visible to those platforms as a result of this connection.

5. Data Security

All connections to DownFiles are encrypted via HTTPS. We do not retain user data beyond what is operationally necessary.

6. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page.

7. Contact

For privacy inquiries, contact us via our Contact page.
